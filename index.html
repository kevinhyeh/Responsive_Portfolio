About Me


Hello my name is Kevin Pak Ho Yeh. I was born in Hong Kong, China on May 17th, 1994. Standing at 6'3", I am is the middle child of two brothers, Jeremy and Lucas. Moving to Foster City, California at age 5, I spent most of my life growing up in the bay.

I grew up with many activities around me. From basketball to golf, I dabbled in majority of sports. Ultimately I stuck with tennis. The mental and phsyical demand need for the sport really interested me.

Due to location of my house, I attended San Mateo High School from 2008-2012. For college, I was accepted to Notre Dame de Namur University, NDNU for short. I recieved my bachelors in Graphic Design with a minor in Film and Photography. During my freetime, I enjoy coding, designing, playing tennis, and sleeping.
